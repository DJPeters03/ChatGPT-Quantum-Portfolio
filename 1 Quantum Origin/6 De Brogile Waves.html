6. De Broglie: Matter as Waves

Quantum Number (n) 3
Wave Amplitude

The Physical Bridge: Bohr said orbits are quantized, but Louis de Broglie explained why.

If the electron is a wave, its path must be a standing wave. If the ends don't meet perfectly, the wave cancels itself out.

Condition: nλ = 2πr

This "integer requirement" is the source of all quantum numbers found in Folder 2.
